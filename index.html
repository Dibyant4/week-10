Flickering Candle

A woman's silhouette illuminated by the gentle, dancing flame of a burning candle

This scene captures the tranquil atmosphere created by a single burning candle. The woman's silhouette in the background represents quiet contemplation, while the animated candle flame brings life to the composition with its natural, flickering motion.

The candle is rendered with realistic wax textures and drips, while the flame uses CSS animations to simulate the natural movement of fire. The warm glow illuminates the silhouette, creating a peaceful and introspective mood.
